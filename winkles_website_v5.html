Our Story
"I watched my father heal using herbs and spices. That moment changed everything for me."

Winkles Nature's Treat Foods was born out of a simple but powerful belief - that we can feed our bodies well without artificial additives or shortcuts.

It started with my father. When he was diagnosed with diabetes, I watched him heal using herbs and spices. That moment changed everything for me. I was already passionate about organic skincare at the time but seeing the power of nature's ingredients up close lit a fire in me that never went out.

When I got married I brought that passion into my kitchen. I started cooking with herbs and spices, tasting and experimenting, learning what each one could do. That curiosity led me to formally study spice blending - where I learned spice profiles, flavour science and how to layer ingredients to create something truly special.

Winkles was born from that journey. Every blend we make carries that same intention - to bring real flavour and real nourishment to your table, the way nature always intended.

Nancy Chinwike
Founder, Winkles Nature's Treat Foods
